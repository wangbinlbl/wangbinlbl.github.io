Presentation on HEVI-LOAD (Heavy-Duty Electric Vehicle Infrastructure - Load Operations and Deployment) focusing on national-scale truck electrification analysis. This seminar discussed the methodology, findings, and implications of large-scale medium- and heavy-duty electric vehicle infrastructure planning.

The presentation covered advanced modeling techniques for predicting charging infrastructure needs, load analysis, and grid integration challenges for commercial vehicle electrification across the United States.
