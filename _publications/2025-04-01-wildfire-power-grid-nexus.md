This comprehensive review examines the interconnected challenges between wildfire risk and electrical grid infrastructure in an era of climate change. The paper explores how changing climate patterns affect both wildfire behavior and power system vulnerability, and discusses strategies for enhancing grid resilience against wildfire threats.
